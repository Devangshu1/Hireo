Hireo is an AI-powered browser extension designed to protect job seekers from scam recruiters and fake job opportunities.

It analyzes incoming emails, job offers, and messages in real-time to identify suspicious patterns such as:
- Generic or automated recruiter messages
- Suspicious links or domains
- Urgency-based or manipulative language
- Repetitive promotional outreach

The goal of Hireo is simple — **help users avoid fake job offers and focus only on genuine opportunities.**

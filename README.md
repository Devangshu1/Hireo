## Hireo – Job Scam Detector (extension)

![Hireo Preview](Img/main.png)

Hireo is an AI-powered browser extension designed to protect job seekers from scam recruiters and fake job opportunities.

It analyzes incoming emails, job offers, and messages in real-time to identify suspicious patterns such as:
- Generic or automated recruiter messages
- Suspicious links or domains
- Urgency-based or manipulative language
- Repetitive promotional outreach

The goal of Hireo is simple — **help users avoid fake job offers and focus only on genuine opportunities.**

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

![Working](Img/scan.png)

Hireo uses AI models and pattern detection techniques to scan content and classify it as safe or potentially harmful.

1. User receives a job-related message/email  
2. Hireo scans the content using AI  
3. It flags suspicious messages instantly  
4. User gets a safer job search experience

## 🎯 Why Hireo?

Job seekers often receive multiple spam or scam messages after applying to jobs online.
